Class: MapBackend

MapBackend()

Abstract mapper backend, i.e. what map is being presented. The backend translates between the concept of a map and a database, a file, an API, or whatever else is actually being used to store the data. Most methods here are low-level; users of the backend should use methods from EntityRef and its children which delegate to the MapBackend.
